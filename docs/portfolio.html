What does a $50k renovation budget get me on a 1,800 sqft house?
Agent calling tools
[tool: estimate_renovation(condition="cosmetic", sqft=1800)] → $22k–$40k · +12% ARV
[tool: estimate_renovation(condition="moderate", sqft=1800)] → $50k–$81k · +25% ARV
Agent
Insight: $50k on 1,800 sqft gets you a full cosmetic renovation with room to spare, or the lower end of a moderate reno — the moderate path delivers 2x the value uplift.

Evidence: Cosmetic ($12–$22/sqft) costs $22k–$40k and lifts ARV by +12%. Moderate ($28–$45/sqft) runs $50k–$81k mid at $66k and lifts ARV by +25%. At $50k budget, cosmetic fits cleanly; moderate requires $16k over budget.

Action: Do the cosmetic reno and bank the remaining $10–28k as a contingency reserve, or find a deal $15k cheaper on acquisition to unlock the moderate tier — the extra 13 points of ARV uplift is worth the stretch if you can create the budget room.
